daydream

There’s no day that I don’t think about
how it’s possible I haven’t been in love
And it always feels fucking far away
the day I’ll hold your hand

I’ve spent way too much time thinking
that there’s a better version of me
And I’m tired of living in this cage
that I’ve built so perfect for myself

Love hard, but it always crashes
Kiss slow, but it’ll turn into ashes

I think I’ll just keep daydreaming
Daydreaming about you and I
I won’t stop until you’re mine
Still trapped inside my mind
But there’s no one to find
So I’ll just keep daydreaming
Daydreaming that your mine

waiting for you

for something that everyone has
will my love be enough?
all the other boys before
have never made me feel the way you do
because you saw a scar in my heart
and didn’t run back

i love the way you try to hide your smile
you’re scared that I’ll break your heart
but I promise, I’ll never make you sad
sometimes i like to think
that I know your mind
and that i know the truth in your heart
so pure and divine

but im tired of waiting
for love
but ill wait, if im waiting
for you
